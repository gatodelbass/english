Blacklist for corrupt politicians / public sarvants

so, what is it?

It is basically an idea with the objective of mitigating the problem of corruption in the government of a country.

The idea was taken from a video of a journalist, which in summary suggests the following:

  • There should be a corrupt blacklist
  • It is so that once identified, they can never get close to public money again
  • One of the problems is that corruption is so daily that there is tolerance or disregard for this phenomenon
  • There are contractors and temporary unions who know and take advantage of the weaknesses of the system to offend again
  • we should isolate those involved with corruption scandals, zero tolerance
  • It is useless even to punish the corrupt if after a few years they go out again to continue stealing state resources
  • indifference cannot be an option when we talk about corruption

Motivation

In Colombia, it is common to see this type of corruption scandal in the news, in contracts between companies and the government. There, multi-million dollar contracts are handled, where money often ends up in the hands of powerful and corrupt people.

These types of corruption scandals are frequent and become a real national shame

This is the money collected from all contributors and citizens (in taxes for example), so should be intended for better health services, education, infraestructure, salaries, etc. Money must go where it should go

How it can work?

This measure should be adopted within the legal framework and must be implemented within national laws.

But first, to get to this point, it must become a collective goal, that is, of all citizens, the media, journalists, educators and even on social networks. Country wide.

perhaps this can begin with spreading the idea among all people, pointing out that it is a good for all. then this can grow

It can finally become an applicable and concrete law
